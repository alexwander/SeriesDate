Web app for creating list of your favorite TV series and tracing air dates for new episodes.

An easy way to see when your favorite TV shows are going to air. 
Shows are presented on a calendar so you can see which day and which episode is airing.
Get notifications of new episodes.
Keep track of what you’ve seen and what you have yet to see.

Technologies: Spring, Hibernate, Twitter Bootstrap, JQuery, Apache Tiles and JSP, Spring Data JPA and Spring Security.
